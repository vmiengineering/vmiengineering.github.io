VISUAL ANALYSIS

IES provides VisualAnalysis to colleges and universities within the USA and Canada to learn about finite element analysis. Hundreds of professors have taught thousands of students about structural analysis with this software, since 1994. Many practicing engineers use the commercial version of VisualAnalysis after learning it in the classroom.

Download Instructions

If you are a student or instructor at a qualified school, with a valid educational email address, you may download the free VisualAnalysis Edu today. Get installed and running in a few minutes. Faculty members please see the setting up a lab instructions.

Students please visit IES website and enter your VMI Email in the "Get VisualAnalysis Educational Now!" text field and click download.

You will receive an email from sales@iesweb.com with the subject "VisualAnalysis Edu Download."  The download link and instructions will be provided.

Instructions:

1. Open the setup-va19-edu.exe
2. Click Finish when complete
3. The Registration Popup will come up. Under school select Virginia Military Institute
4. Input your name 
5. Input your VMI email 
6. Click Register
