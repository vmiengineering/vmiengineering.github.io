Educational Software

Many of our software vendors offer educational/academic software packages that are free for use for Engineering students and faculty.  Below you will find a list of all the software currently available for downloads as a student/cadet or faculty of the Engineering departments of VMI.

Autodesk (AutoCAD, Revit, Fusion 360, Civil, EAGLE...)

    AUTODESK offers free educational software for educators and students. Please goto https://www.autodesk.com/education/free-software/featured to see all software available.

    You must sign up for an account with AUTODESK. Once you create an account sign in and select the version of software, operating system, and language you want.     

        Please follow the directions provided at the website on how to install, if you have issues installing please contact AUTODESK support here or go to the Barracks Help Desk located in C01-B in New Barracks for assistance, or you can see the Divisional Engineering IT Specialist in NEB 406.
