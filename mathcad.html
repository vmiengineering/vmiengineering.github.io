Educational Software

Many of our software vendors offer educational/academic software packages that are free for use for Engineering students and faculty.  Below you will find a list of all the software currently available for downloads as a student/cadet or faculty of the Engineering departments of VMI.

MathCAD 15.0 M045 & MathCAD Express

MathCAD 15.0 M045 [ME ONLY - PURCHASE ONLY]

    The Mechanical Engineering department holds several standalone licenses for MathCAD 15.0 M045.
To purchase a stand alone license please see Charlene Graves to purchase the Product Code ($20)
Once product code is paid for Charlene will notify the Divisional Engineering IT Specialist, then he will send an email to you with directions and your product code .

- Make sure to keep track of your serial number that will be sent to you
- Documentation for MathCAD 15.0 M045 can be found here

MathCAD Express (FREE for students and educators)

    PTC offers free-for-life Engineering Math Software. PTC Mathcad Express is free math software that gives lifetime access to selected features of PTC Mathcad Prime 6.0

- Click here to goto https://www.mathcad.com/en/try-and-buy/mathcad-express-free-download to sign up and download the software
- Please note that MathCAD Express may not have all the features that are available in MathCAD 15.0 M045 (version used in computer labs), please refer to the comparison chart to see the differences in features of MathCAD Express compared to the version 15.0 M045 documentation found here
