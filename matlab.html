Educational Software

Many of our software vendors offer educational/academic software packages that are free for use for Engineering students and faculty.  Below you will find a list of all the software currently available for downloads as a student/cadet or faculty of the Engineering departments of VMI.

MatLab & Simulink

Matlab and Simulink is now offered to the whole campus for all through Matlabs Online Portal/Campus-wide license. Students/cadets are covered by the campus-wide license and can install software on their home or laptop computers.

TO ACCESS THE MATLAB PORTAL FOR VMI CLICK HERE

- Click "Sign in to get started"
- This will take you to the MathWorks account login screen. Please login. If you do not have an account click the "Create Account" link found on the page. Please sign up using your VMI email when creating an account .      You must have a Mathworks account to download MatLab.
- Once you create your account you will be associated to our Matlab license and will be able to download and activate software on your personal computer and also gain access to start using Matlab Online from a web browser
- If you are not familiar with Matlab please complete the 2 hour Matlab Onramp tutorial that can be found on the initial portal site by scrolling down and clicking "Start learning now" in the MATLAB Onramp box

1.  DOWNLOAD THE INSTALLER

- Go to Matlab Portal and click "Sign in to get started" then login with your Mathworks account you have created
- Click the download buttom for the current release. (Users can also download previous releases here)
- Choose a supported platform and download the installer

2.  INSTALL AND ACTIVATE

- Run the installer
- In the installer, select Log in with a MathWorks Account and follow the online instructions
- When prompted to do so, select the Academic - Total Headcount lcense labeled individual
- Select the products you want ot download and install
- After downloading and installing your products, keep the Activate MATLAB checkbox xelected and click Next
- Follow the prompts to activate MATLAB

MATLAB ONLINE - Can be used online through web browser

- No downloads needed
- No setup needed
- No installation needed
- No maintenance to maintain
- Just sign up and sign in
- Use it anywhere, anytime, on any computer, laptop or Chromebook
- To access Matlab Online goto https://matlab.mathworks.com/
- Matlab is also available for mobile devices: iPhone, iPad, or Android

INSTALLATION OR PROGRAM ISSUES?

Contact MathWorks Support" to create a support ticket.   Alternatively you can goto the Barracks Help Desk located in C01-B in New Barracks for assistance, or if you are an engineering cadet you can see the Divisional Engineering IT Specialist in NEB 406.
